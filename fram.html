Virtual Private Network (VPN) is the system used to remotely connect to the Banner Health network. A VPN provides an encrypted secure connection between your off-site device and the Banner Health network. Only employees requiring VPN access and using Banner Health devices, are permitted. If you need assistance with VPN, contact the IT Service Desk.
